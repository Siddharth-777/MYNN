How to Use MYNN

1. Upload Your Paper: Select a research paper in PDF format. MYNN will parse and understand its content.

2. Ask Questions: Type any question about the paper's content, methodology, or implications.

3. Get Insights: MYNN's models analyze your question and provide comprehensive answers.

Example Questions

You can ask MYNN questions like:

  • "What is the main hypothesis of this paper?"
  • "How does the methodology work?"
  • "What are the key findings?"
  • "Can you explain this concept in simple terms?"
  • "What are the limitations of this study?"
  • "How does this research compare to previous work?"

Tips for Better Results

  • Be Specific: Ask detailed questions rather than vague ones for more precise answers
  • Reference Content: Mention specific sections, figures, or concepts from the paper
  • Ask for Explanations: Request step-by-step explanations of complex concepts or methods
  • Explore Connections: Ask about relationships between different parts of the research
  • Use Technical Terms: MYNN understands academic language and can explain it clearly
