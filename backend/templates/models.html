About MYNN

MYNN (Multi-Modal Neural Network) is an advanced AI platform designed to help you understand, analyze, and interact with research papers. Inspired by the Star Wars universe, MYNN brings together multiple specialized models to provide deep insights, creative connections, and logical reasoning for your academic documents.

What is MYNN?

MYNN combines multiple specialized AI models to provide comprehensive analysis of research papers. It's designed to help researchers, students, and academics understand complex papers quickly and thoroughly. Named after the neural networks in Star Wars, MYNN brings the same level of sophistication to research analysis.

Our platform understands your uploaded document and uses it as context for every answer, ensuring relevance and accuracy in all responses.

Our Models

EmoNet

Analyzes the emotional tone and sentiment of research content, helping understand the authors' perspective and the overall mood of the paper.

LogicNet

Provides step-by-step logical reasoning and answers complex analytical questions with clear explanations and evidence-based responses.

MemoryNet

Retrieves relevant information from your uploaded document to provide contextually accurate answers based on the specific content.

CreativeNet

Generates creative analogies, connections, and insights to help you see research in new ways and make unexpected discoveries.

Cortex Fusion

Intelligently combines outputs from all models to generate comprehensive, well-structured answers that leverage the strengths of each model.

How It Works

1. Document Upload: Upload your research paper as a PDF. Our system parses and understands the content structure.

2. Question Processing: Your question is analyzed and routed to the most appropriate models for comprehensive analysis.

3. Model Analysis: Each model processes your question using the document context and generates specialized insights.

4. Fusion & Response: The Cortex Fusion model combines all insights into a coherent, comprehensive answer for you.
